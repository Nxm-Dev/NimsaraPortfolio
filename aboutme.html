About Me

Hello! I’m Nimsara Jayathilaka, a dedicated Computer Science undergraduate currently pursuing my third year at the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT). From a young age, I’ve been fascinated by the intersection of science and technology. My academic journey began at Kegalu Vidyalaya, where I developed a deep passion for exploring the world of science, technology, and innovation. During my school years, I not only focused on academics but also actively engaged in a variety of extracurricular activities, which helped shape my character and leadership skills. I served as Deputy Head Prefect, a role that not only developed my leadership abilities but also instilled in me a sense of responsibility and an unwavering commitment to my peers. Beyond leadership, I was an active member of the Senior Brass Band and Drama Club, where I further honed my teamwork and communication skills. These experiences taught me the importance of collaboration, discipline, and perseverance—qualities that I bring into every professional project and endeavor I undertake today.

In 2021, I completed my A-levels with distinction in the science stream, which opened the doors to my selection for the prestigious IIT Infoschol program. This opportunity was a pivotal moment in my academic and professional journey, marking the beginning of my specialized focus in technology and software development. Since then, I have immersed myself in the world of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems, cloud solutions, and business process automation, gaining hands-on experience and developing a deep understanding of complex software architectures. My academic and practical experiences have provided me with the tools and knowledge to become proficient in ERP solutions, with a strong focus on Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. Over the past few years, I’ve been actively involved in developing and implementing Business Central solutions, which have empowered businesses to streamline their operations and maximize their efficiency. I am passionate about creating innovative solutions that solve complex business problems, and I am driven by the opportunity to turn ideas into impactful, real-world applications. I specialize in a range of Microsoft 365 technologies, including Power Apps, Power Automate, and Azure DevOps, with a strong emphasis on Agile methodologies. These tools allow me to design and deliver end-to-end business solutions that are scalable, efficient, and adaptable to the ever-changing needs of today’s digital landscape. My expertise in integrating platforms such as Shopify with Business Central has also provided businesses with seamless, cross-functional workflows that connect their eCommerce operations with ERP, enhancing their overall productivity.

I pride myself on being a progressive thinker who thrives on working across all stages of product development, from the initial ideation phase to design, development, and eventual deployment. I enjoy the challenge of taking a problem, thinking creatively, and developing solutions that not only address the current needs but also anticipate future requirements. Whether I am building powerful business intelligence tools, working on integration solutions, or optimizing business workflows, I always approach each project with the same level of commitment and passion for excellence. Through this portfolio, I aim to showcase my skills, projects, and professional experiences, all of which reflect my dedication to innovation and my passion for technology. Each project is a testament to my commitment to delivering exceptional value and results. As I continue to grow in my career, I am eager to collaborate with like-minded professionals and organizations to create solutions that drive meaningful change and push the boundaries of what technology can achieve.
