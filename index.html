About Me

Curious about me? Here you have it:

Hello! I'm Nimsara Jayathilaka, a passionate Computer Science undergraduate currently in my third year at IIT. My academic journey began at Kegalu Vidyalaya, where I cultivated my love for science and technology. During my school years, I actively participated in a variety of extracurricular activities, including being a Deputy Head Prefect and an active member of the Senior Brass Band and the Drama Club. These experiences not only honed my leadership and communication skills but also instilled a deep sense of teamwork and discipline that I carry with me in my professional life today.

In 2021, after completing my A-levels with distinction in the science stream, I was selected for the prestigious IIT Infoschol program. This marked the beginning of my focused journey in the world of technology. Over the past few years, I have gained extensive knowledge and practical experience in software development, particularly in ERP solutions. My passion for solving complex problems and creating innovative technology solutions continues to drive me forward. Whether it's developing efficient ERP systems, working on integration solutions, or building powerful business intelligence tools, I am always eager to learn and grow in the ever-evolving tech landscape. Through this portfolio, I aim to showcase my skills, projects, and professional experiences that reflect my commitment to excellence and my passion for technology. I look forward to connecting and collaborating with like-minded professionals and organizations as I continue my journey in the tech world.
